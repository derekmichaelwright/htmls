Data Sources

  • FAO Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations fao.org/faostat/
  • STATCAN Statistics Canada statcan.gc.ca/
  • USDA United States Department of Agriculture usda.gov/
  • ISAAA International Service for the Acquisition of Agri-biotech Applications isaaa.org/

Instalation

devtools::install_github("derekmichaelwright/agData")
library(agData)

This will also the load the following packages: tidyverse, ggarrange, ggpubr & ggrepel.

theme_agData

xx <- data.frame(x = 1:10, y = 1:10, color = rep(c("1","2"), times = 2, each = 5))
mp <- ggplot(xx, aes(x = x, y = y, color = color)) + 
  geom_point(size = 4) + facet_grid(.~color)
mp

mp + theme_agData()

mp + theme_agData(legend.position = "none")

mp + theme_agData(linesize = 1.5, rotx = T,
                  bgFill = alpha("darkgreen",0.2), 
                  stripFill = alpha("darkred",0.2),
                  lineColor = "darkorange")
